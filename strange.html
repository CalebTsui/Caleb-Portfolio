STRANGE SOUNDS FROM BEYOND EXPRESSIVE MICROSITE

Encourage exploration of Strange Sounds from Beyond’s unorthodox essence

Overview

The project was developed for IAT 235: Information Design. The project was centered around the topic, which the team extensively researched through the lens of relevant precedents. This all culminated into an expressive microsite, guided by our design qualities, principles, and graphical experimentation. *This project is not affiliated with Strange Sounds from Beyond. It is a case study done for an academic project.*

Contributions:

Content Design, Research, Visual Design

Team:

Joseph Lee, Michelle Lee, Christina Lee

Duration:

About the Client

Strange Sounds From Beyond is a music festival in Amsterdam that celebrates diverse genres of music, drawing music enthusiasts seeking unconventional and unique sounds. The result is an immersive experience that unites different music cultures.
